Bleeding during pregnancy requires immediate attention Any vaginal bleeding during pregnancy should be evaluated promptly by a healthcare provider or in an emergency setting. This tool is not designed for assessment of bleeding during active pregnancy.
If you are experiencing heavy bleeding, severe cramping, dizziness, or passed tissue — please go to the emergency room or call 911 immediately.

If your bleeding is light and you would still like to complete this assessment for research documentation purposes, you may continue. Your care team will be notified of your response.
